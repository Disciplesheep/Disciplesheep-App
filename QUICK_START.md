# Sow & Reap Journal - Quick Start Guide

## What's Included

### ✅ Pre-filled Daily Devotionals
- **Passage**: NASB Bible verses relevant to church planting (365 days)
- **Principle**: Timeless truths for ministry (read-only, pre-written)
- **Practice**: Specific daily action steps for Puerto Princesa context (pre-written)
- **Praises**: Your personal gratitude journal (you fill this)
- **Prayer**: Your daily intercession (you fill this)

### ✅ Goals & Progress Tracking
The journal automatically tracks your progress:
- **People Contacted**: Target 10/month (updates as you add contacts)
- **Budget Used**: Target ₱11,400/month (updates as you log expenses)
- **Year 1 Goals**: Identify 5 Timothys, reach 120 people, launch Bible study

### ✅ Live Features
Every time you add a person or expense, the progress bars update in real-time!

## How to Use Daily

1. **Open Journal** → Navigate to today's date
2. **Read** the pre-filled Passage, Principle, Practice
3. **Check off** your daily tasks
4. **Write** your Praises and Prayer
5. **Log** any people contacted or expenses
6. **Watch** your progress bars update!

## Sample Daily Flow

**Morning (10 min)**
- Read today's devotional passage
- Apply the practice step for today
- Write your praises & prayers
- Check off: "Scripture done"

**Throughout Day**
- Meet someone? → Add to People Tracker
- Spend money? → Add to Expense Ledger
- Complete task? → Check it off

**Evening (5 min)**
- Review today's goals card
- Check weekly report on Sunday
- Celebrate progress!
